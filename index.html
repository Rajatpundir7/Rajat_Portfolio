Essay Writing – Structured Notes

1. Purpose & Technique

Develops critical thinking, subject knowledge, and communication skills.

  • Understand: Identify keywords and purpose.
  • Brainstorm: Think of points, examples, arguments.
  • Structure: Introduction, Body, Conclusion.

2. Types of Essays

📝 Descriptive: Describes a person, place, or event in detail using sensory details.
📖 Narrative: Tells a story or shares personal experiences chronologically.
📊 Expository: Explains or informs about a topic based on objective facts and examples.
⚖️ Argumentative: Presents a clear opinion or argument with logical reasoning and evidence.

3. Essay Structure

  • Introduction: Hook (interesting start), Background of topic, Thesis statement (main idea).
  • Body Paragraphs: One idea per paragraph. Use examples, facts, explanations.
  • Conclusion: Summarize key points, restate main idea, end with a strong closing thought.
